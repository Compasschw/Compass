# CompassCHW Privacy Policy

**Effective:** April 18, 2026
**Entity:** CompassCHW, LLC ("CompassCHW", "we", "us", "our")
**Contact:** privacy@joincompasschw.com · joincompasschw.com/contact

This Privacy Policy describes how CompassCHW collects, uses, shares, and protects
information when you use our Services — including our website, mobile apps, and
any related platforms (collectively, the "Services").

---

## 1. Who We Are

CompassCHW is a two-sided care-coordination platform connecting Community Health
Workers (CHWs) with Medi-Cal members in Los Angeles County. We operate as a
Business Associate under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
(HIPAA) when processing Protected Health Information (PHI) on behalf of Covered
Entities such as Medi-Cal Managed Care Plans.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly
- **Identity & contact:** name, email, phone, mailing address
- **CHW credentials:** certifications, specializations, languages, years of experience
- **Member health information:** Medi-Cal ID, insurance provider, primary needs
  (housing, food, mental health, etc.), service requests, and session notes
- **Consent records:** signatures, IP address, user-agent, timestamp

### Information collected automatically
- **Session metadata:** timestamps, duration, participant IDs, billing units
- **Audit logs:** actions taken, resources accessed, IP address, user-agent
- **Device information:** device type, OS version, app version, push tokens
- **Communication metadata:** masked phone number routing, call duration, recording
  timestamps (we do not collect the real phone numbers of the opposite party)

### Information we do NOT collect
- Location data except as voluntarily provided (ZIP code for CHW matching)
- Contacts, photos, or files outside of what you explicitly upload
- Biometric data
- Tracking identifiers across third-party apps or websites

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Deliver the Services** — match CHWs with members, schedule sessions, enable
  communication, process Medi-Cal billing
- **Maintain records** — required for Medi-Cal claims (7-year retention under
  22 CCR §51476) and HIPAA audit controls (45 CFR §164.312(b))
- **Improve quality** — analyze aggregated, de-identified metrics to improve
  matching and outcomes
- **Communicate with you** — session reminders, request notifications, service
  announcements, and security alerts
- **Compliance and safety** — detect fraud, prevent abuse, respond to legal process

We do NOT sell your personal information. We do NOT use your PHI for marketing.

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We share information only as described below:

| Recipient | Purpose | Basis |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Pear Suite | Medi-Cal claims submission and adjudication | HIPAA BAA |
| Vonage | Masked phone calling and SMS between CHW and member | HIPAA BAA |
| AssemblyAI | Transcription of recorded sessions (with consent) | HIPAA BAA |
| Amazon Web Services | Cloud hosting of the application and database | HIPAA BAA |
| California Medi-Cal managed care plans | Submission of claims for reimbursement | HIPAA authorization; minimum necessary |
| Your CHW or Member counterpart | Within an active session context | Your authorization when creating the session |

We do not share data with advertising networks, data brokers, or social platforms.

## 5. Your Rights

Under HIPAA and California law (including CMIA, CCPA, and CPRA):

- **Access:** request a copy of your PHI — handled per 45 CFR §164.524
- **Correction:** request amendment of PHI we maintain — per §164.526
- **Accounting of disclosures:** request a list of disclosures made about you — per §164.528
- **Deletion:** request deletion of your account — available in-app via
  Settings → Delete My Account, or by emailing privacy@joincompasschw.com
- **Portability:** request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restrict uses:** request limits on how we use your PHI — per §164.522
- **Complain:** file a complaint with us, the U.S. Department of Health &
  Human Services (HHS), or the California Attorney General

To exercise any right, contact privacy@joincompasschw.com. We respond within
30 days (60 days for complex requests).

### Account deletion details
When you delete your account:
- Your profile, email, phone, and name are pseudonymized within 24 hours
- Your Medi-Cal ID and other PHI are irreversibly encrypted with keys we no
  longer possess, making the data inaccessible
- Billing and session records are retained for the 7-year Medi-Cal window
  (22 CCR §51476) in pseudonymized form, then permanently deleted
- Refresh tokens are revoked immediately on all devices

## 6. Security

We implement the HIPAA Security Rule's Administrative, Physical, and Technical
Safeguards (45 CFR §§164.308, 164.310, 164.312), including:

- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256-GCM for sensitive PHI fields (Medi-Cal ID)
  plus AWS RDS disk encryption for the full database
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.2+ for all network communication
- **Access controls:** role-based authentication, least-privilege by default,
  minimum-necessary redaction on shared views
- **Audit logging:** every data access is logged with user ID, IP, action, and
  resource; logs retained for 7 years
- **Workforce training:** all team members complete HIPAA training before
  accessing any real member data
- **Business Associate Agreements:** signed with every vendor that processes
  PHI on our behalf

No system is perfectly secure. We notify affected individuals and regulators
within 60 days of any Breach as defined at 45 CFR §164.402.

## 7. Retention

- **Active account data:** retained while your account is active plus 7 years
  after last activity (Medi-Cal requirement)
- **Audit logs:** 7 years minimum
- **Session recordings and transcripts:** 7 years, then permanently deleted
- **Marketing / analytics:** 2 years (aggregated, non-identifying data)
- **Cookies and analytics identifiers:** 13 months

## 8. Children

The Services are not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child
under 13 has provided us information, please contact privacy@joincompasschw.com
and we will delete it promptly.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes at least 30 days before they take effect
via email and in-app notice. Continued use after the effective date of a revised
policy means you accept the changes.

## 10. California Residents

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy
Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). Most of these rights are
already provided above under HIPAA. To submit a CCPA request, email
privacy@joincompasschw.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.
