# HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

**Effective:** April 18, 2026
**CompassCHW, LLC**

---

## Our Commitment

CompassCHW operates as a **Business Associate** under HIPAA when processing
Protected Health Information (PHI) on behalf of Covered Entities (Medi-Cal
managed care plans, healthcare providers, and community-based organizations).
This Notice describes how we may use and disclose your PHI and your rights
regarding that information.

## PHI We May Handle

- Your name, contact information, and Medi-Cal ID
- Your self-reported health and social needs
- Session notes documented by Community Health Workers (CHWs)
- Call recordings and transcripts (only with your explicit consent)
- Billing claims submitted on your behalf

## How We May Use and Disclose PHI

**For Treatment, Payment, and Health Care Operations (§164.506)**
- Coordinating care between you and your CHW
- Submitting Medi-Cal claims for reimbursement
- Quality assurance reviews of session documentation

**As Required by Law (§164.512)**
- Court orders, subpoenas, or similar judicial process
- Reports to public health authorities for reportable conditions
- Response to HHS Office for Civil Rights compliance investigations

**With Your Authorization (§164.508)**
- Sharing with a specific third party you name in writing
- Use in research (only after IRB approval and your signed authorization)
- Marketing communications (never without your explicit written authorization)

**We will NEVER**
- Sell your PHI
- Use your PHI for advertising
- Share your PHI with data brokers or social platforms

## Your Rights Under HIPAA

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| Access your PHI (§164.524) | Email privacy@joincompasschw.com; response within 30 days |
| Request correction (§164.526) | Email with "Amendment Request" in subject; specify what should be corrected |
| Accounting of disclosures (§164.528) | Request a list of disclosures in the last 6 years |
| Restrict certain uses (§164.522) | Request a restriction; we'll tell you if we can accommodate |
| Confidential communication (§164.522) | Request that we contact you only via specific channels |
| Paper copy of this Notice | Available by email on request |
| File a complaint | With us, or with HHS OCR at hhs.gov/hipaa/filing-a-complaint |

## Minimum Necessary Standard

When we use or disclose your PHI, we follow HIPAA's minimum-necessary standard
(§164.514(d)): we limit disclosures to the smallest amount of information
needed for the purpose. For example, when CHWs browse open service requests,
they see only the service category, urgency, and mode — never your name or
description — until they accept your request.

## Breach Notification

If a Breach of your unsecured PHI occurs, we will notify you within 60 days
as required by 45 CFR §164.404. Notification will include:
- A description of what happened and what information was involved
- Steps you can take to protect yourself
- What we are doing to investigate and mitigate
- Contact information for follow-up

## Business Associate Agreements

We have signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with every vendor that
processes your PHI on our behalf. Current list:

- **Amazon Web Services** (cloud hosting)
- **Pear Suite** (Medi-Cal claims submission)
- **Vonage** (masked calling and SMS) *(BAA pending)*
- **AssemblyAI** (medical transcription)

If any vendor relationship changes, this Notice will be updated.

## Changes to This Notice

We may revise this Notice. Revisions apply to all PHI we maintain. The
effective date at the top of this document is always current. We post
material changes in-app and on our website at least 30 days before they
take effect.
